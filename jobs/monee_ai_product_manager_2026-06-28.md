### Full Job Description
About The Team
Drive the future of data-driven decision-making by building AI-powered products that transform our business. You'll own the product roadmap for AI-enabled identifying high-impact opportunities and shipping solutions that measurably improve speed, quality, and cost-efficiency.
Job Description
Own the AI roadmap – Define and execute the product strategy for AI-driven tools, aligned with business and data strategy
Identify and solve BI challenges – Pinpoint where AI can unlock value in reporting, self-service analytics, and process automation
Build products from concept to launch – Partner with stakeholders, data engineers, and analysts to design solutions, define requirements, and deliver on time
Drive measurable impact – Launch pilots, scale adoption, and track metrics (adoption rates, query efficiency, cost savings, time-to-insight) to prove ROI
Champion AI adoption – Help teams integrate AI into daily workflows and build an AI-first culture
Requirements
2+ years product management experience — or fresh graduates with strong fundamentals and genuine hunger to learn
AI/LLM fundamentals - Understanding of prompt engineering, agentic AI concepts, and working knowledge of LLM frameworks
Strong BI fundamentals – Understanding of reporting systems, data modeling, and analytics platforms
Stakeholder management skills – Ability to align and influence across business, engineering, and data teams
Data-driven problem solver – Analytical mindset with proven ability to drive decisions with data
Self-starter mentality – Comfortable owning initiatives independently from ideation to launch
Nice To Have
Experience with AI/LLM technologies, AI agents, or analytics automation
Familiarity with LLM orchestration frameworks
