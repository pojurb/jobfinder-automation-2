### Full Job Description
This is a confidential search. Company name will be revealed during the first round of interviews.
Lead the strategy, definition, and delivery of our digital health platform. Own the product roadmap across member-facing experiences, care navigation, and AI-powered health tools — translating clinical and business needs into elegant, compliant, and scalable product solutions. Oversee the Content development and UX/UI functions. This is a high-impact, hands-on leadership role ideal for someone passionate about behavioral health, data-driven product development, and building in a fast-moving environment
Responsibilities:
Own and drive the product roadmap across one or more platform domains, balancing near-term delivery with long-term strategic vision.
Partner closely with engineering, design, clinical, and data teams to define, prioritize, and ship high-quality product experiences.
Lead discovery efforts including user research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis to deeply understand member, provider, and client needs.
Author clear, detailed Product Requirements Documents (PRDs), user stories, and acceptance criteria that empower engineering teams to execute with confidence.
Translate complex healthcare workflows and compliance requirements (HIPAA, SOC 2, HITRUST) into pragmatic product decisions and feature definitions.
Define and track product success metrics — establishing KPIs, instrumentation requirements, and feedback loops to drive continuous improvement.
Align cross-functional stakeholders on priorities, tradeoffs, and launch readiness.
Champion AI-powered product capabilities including care navigation agents, member-facing assistants, and intelligent content delivery.
Manage and prioritize the product backlog in Jira; participate actively in agile ceremonies and sprint planning.
Lead content development and UX/UI teams. Build, mentor, and develop high-performing teams.
Education/Experience: 
High School Diploma/GED required
Bachelor degree preferred or relevant experience in lieu of degree
5+ years of product management experience in B2B SaaS, digital health, or healthcare technology.
Demonstrated ability to lead full product lifecycle from discovery through delivery and iteration.
Strong command of writing PRDs, defining user stories, and working within agile development frameworks.
Experience working in regulated environments with knowledge of HIPAA, healthcare data privacy, or health plan operations.
Comfort with technical concepts including APIs, microservices, data pipelines, and AI/ML product capabilities — enough to partner credibly with engineers.
Proven ability to influence without authority across engineering, clinical, and business stakeholders.
Experience with Jira, Figma, and collaborative tools in a remote-first environment.
Familiarity with AI tools and a desire to leverage them to accelerate product development, discovery, and documentation workflows.
Background in behavioral health, mental health, or whole-person care platforms is a strong plus.
