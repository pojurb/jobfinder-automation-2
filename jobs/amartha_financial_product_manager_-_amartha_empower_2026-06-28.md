### Full Job Description
About Amartha
Amartha is a technology company focused on creating shared prosperity by developing digital financial solutions for the grassroots economy. Founded in 2010 as a microfinance institution, Amartha connects rural, women-led micro-enterprises with affordable capital. Amartha is expanding as a tech company, building a microfinance ecosystem that connects to the growing digital economy through capital, investment, and payment services. By boosting the competitiveness of micro and small entrepreneurs, Amartha empowers women-led MSMEs, creates jobs, and fosters inclusive economic growth.
Amartha Empower is evolving into an integrated impact platform within the Amartha ecosystem, with ambitions to scale donation, zakat, and wakaf through digital channels.
About The Role
The Product Manager will be responsible for owning the end-to-end development of Amartha Empower's digital platform, ensuring a seamless, intuitive, and high-converting user experience.
This role bridges:
Business (Zakat Lead, Partnerships, Campaigns)
Engineering (Web, Mobile, QA)
Growth & Marketing
Responsibilities
 Product Strategy & Roadmap
Define and own product vision aligned with Empower & Amartha goals
Develop and prioritize product roadmap based on business impact
 User Experience & Conversion Optimization
Improve end-to-end donor journey:
Optimize conversion rate and reduce drop-offs
 Feature Development & Execution
Lead development of key features:
Translate business requirements into clear product specs (PRD)
Work closely with Engineering for delivery
 Data-Driven Product Iteration
Define and track key product metrics:
 Conversion rate
 Funnel drop-off
 Retention & repeat donation
Run experiments (A/B testing, feature iteration)
Use insights to continuously improve product performance
 Cross-functional Collaboration
Act as translator between business and tech
Requirements
 3-6+ Years Product Management Experience
Experience in consumer product / fintech / marketplace / platform
Proven track record of shipping impactful features
 Strong Product Thinking
Ability to break down problems into clear product solutions
Strong sense of UX, user behavior, and funnel optimization
 Data-Driven Mindset
Comfortable working with metrics, dashboards, and experiments
Able to translate data into product decisions
 Technical Understanding
Able to work effectively with engineers
Understand basic system design
 Mission Alignment (Strong Plus)
Interest in social impact / donation / financial inclusion
Understanding of zakat ecosystem is a bonus
