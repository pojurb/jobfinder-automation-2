About ClearOne Advantage
Founded in 2008, ClearOne Advantage has helped tens of thousands of clients resolve large credit card bills and other unsecured debts – all without upfront fees or the need for personal bankruptcy. Our main office is in Baltimore, Maryland, and our 500+ employee workforce operates fully remotely. Working in the direct-to-consumer financial services industry, we are dedicated to treating all people with respect and empathy.
About the Role
We’re seeking a Senior Product Manager to own the end-to-end digital experience from first click through early client lifecycle driving measurable outcomes by reducing friction, improving conversion and engagement, and ensuring experiences are observable, compliant, and scalable
This is a 0-to-1 role. You’ll lead the vision, strategy, and execution for a modern self-enrollment funnel, a re-imagined client portal, and a mobile experience we’re defining together. Some of these experiences are net new; others will be re-imagined from existing foundations. Either way, you’ll set the bar for what an empathetic, conversion-driven digital experience looks like in our industry and help raise the craft of the broader product team along the way.
What You’ll Need:
Set the 0-to-1 Vision
: Lead the vision, strategy, and roadmap for our portfolio of digital experiences self-enrollment, client portal, and mobile turning ambiguity into a clear, sequenced plan that ties to business outcomes
Lead Digital Self-Enrollment First
: Take point on our highest-priority initiative — modernizing the consumer-facing self-enrollment experience so prospective clients can confidently move from interest to enrolled, on their own terms
Champion Funnel & Conversion Optimization
: Use experimentation, A/B testing, and rapid prototyping to lift conversion at every step of the funnel — from first touch through enrollment and ongoing engagement
Obsess Over the Client Experience
: Ground every decision in real client research and behavioral data, ensuring our digital touchpoints feel intuitive, transparent, and aligned with our mission to help clients become debt-free
Mentor the Product Bench
: Operate as a player-coach — own your product portfolio while elevating the craft of junior PMs and APMs through coaching, frameworks, and reviews
Partner Across the Business
: Align engineering, design, marketing, operations, legal/compliance, and executive stakeholders around shared outcomes; bring everyone along on the why, the what, and the trade-offs
Measure What Matters
: Establish clear success metrics for each initiative, instrument the experiences, and ensure every release ties back to client outcomes and business impact
Build for Observability and Scale
: Partner with engineering to ensure every digital experience is properly instrumented, monitored, and architected to scale as our client base grows
Build for Compliance by Design
: Partner closely with legal, compliance, and operations to ensure every experience meets regulatory requirements without sacrificing client experience
Own Agile Delivery
: Lead core product ceremonies — backlog grooming, prioritization, planning, and retrospectives — to keep the pod moving with rhythm and quality
