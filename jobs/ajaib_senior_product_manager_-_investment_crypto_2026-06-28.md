### Full Job Description
Life at Ajaib
Here at Ajaib, we address ourselves as Magicians because making magic happen is our specialty! Ajaib is committed to creating an inclusive environment where all Magicians are valued for their contributions, opinions, and skills. Through our 3DNAs, we implement Everyone is an Owner to strengthen our core value in developing as a team, Growth Mindset to never stop learning, and A Will to Win to have courage in being excellent in everything we do!
Job Description
Lead new investment and crypto product launch in collaboration with compliance, regulators and external partners. Clarify regulatory requirements, build proof-of-concept products, obtain new product approval for regulators
Participate in annual audit exercises. Being able to explain to regulators on of the company's investment product end-to-end technical design and operation procedures
Managing the entire lifecycle of product development, from conceptualization to launch and ongoing optimization. This part includes a collaboration with various teams such as compliance, engineering, design, marketing, and business team to ensure the timely and successful delivery of products
Collaborate across teams and contribute insights, ideas, and features into crafting a cohesive product roadmap strategy
Assessing and mitigating risks associated with investment products, such as liquidity risk, operational risk and regulatory compliance
Analyze data to create recommendations and execute product experiments to improve key metrics
Requirements
Min 5 years of experience in product development and having a deep understanding of financial markets, investment products and cash management. Proven track record of successfully launching and managing products from concept to commercialization
Min 2 years experience working with regulators and auditors in the financial industry. Preferably having direct working experience with IDX, OJK, KSEI, KPEI
Superb communication and interpersonal skills. Able to engage external stakeholders diplomatically. Build trust while discerning and tactfully addressing underlying agendas from partners
Strong analytical skills with the ability to interpret data, draw insights, and make data-driven decisions
Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, IT, Electrical Engineering, Industrial Engineering, similar technical field of study or equivalent practical experience
Proficient in Bahasa Indonesia
Benefits
Join us as we make magic happen to increase Indonesia's financial inclusion!
