### Full Job Description
About Adly:
Adly is a technology, data, and performance-driven portfolio of SaaS subscription and content companies. Our founders have built, acquired, scaled, and sold SaaS, ecommerce, and marketing businesses - and continue adding companies to the Adly portfolio.
We scale our businesses through a combination of paid media, organic search/SEO, CRO, and product-led growth - and we are increasingly leveraging AI across product development, operations, and decision-making.
A Product Manager at Adly owns product outcomes across multiple businesses in the portfolio. You will drive the full product lifecycle - from discovery and prioritization through engineering execution, launch, and performance analysis - for several products simultaneously. You are the connective tissue between engineering, growth, design, and operations, ensuring every sprint, feature, and release ladders up to business KPIs.
This is a high-autonomy, high-ownership role. You set the priorities, run the process, drive accountability, analyze the data, and present the results to leadership. You operate with a business owner mindset because, functionally, that is what you are - the person responsible for the trajectory of the products you manage.
You are a builder first. You prototype with AI coding tools (Cursor, Claude Code, Bolt), run evals on AI-powered features, and ship faster because you can go from idea to working proof-of-concept without waiting for engineering. You don't just manage products - you make things.
Responsibilities:
Own the product roadmap and backlog across multiple Adly portfolio companies, prioritizing based on business impact, urgency, and resource constraints
Lead engineering teams through planning, execution, and delivery - keeping projects on track, removing blockers, and maintaining velocity
Collaborate with Growth, Design, and Operations stakeholders to scope requirements, align on goals, and translate business needs into actionable engineering work
Define success metrics for every initiative and rigorously measure outcomes post-launch - revenue, retention, conversion, engagement, LTV
Plan and execute A/B tests, new conversion funnels, landing pages, and growth experiments with measurable results
QA product launches to ensure features ship bug-free and meet stakeholder requirements
Monitor product performance closely, making fast decisions to iterate, scale, or revert based on data
Present performance, progress, insights, and recommendations to leadership with clarity and conviction
Evaluate where AI/LLM features can create step-function improvements in user experience, conversion, or operational efficiency across portfolio products
Build and maintain AI-powered workflows for your own PM practice - competitive analysis, user research synthesis, data exploration, and spec generation
Stay current with AI model capabilities, agentic frameworks, and developer tools - and proactively share and potentially apply new capabilities to your products and processes
Skills & Experience:
2+ years of product management experience, ideally in a fast-paced or multi-product environment
You have managed engineering teams and shipped products end-to-end - from scoping through launch and iteration
Strong analytical skills - you are comfortable pulling data, building reports, running experiments, and making decisions grounded in metrics
Proficiency with project management tools (Asana or similar) and a system for keeping complex, parallel workstreams organized
Experience with marketing funnels, CRO, and A/B testing
Excellent communicator - you translate between technical and non-technical stakeholders and keep everyone aligned
Demonstrated AI power user - you can show specific examples of using AI tools (LLMs, coding assistants, automation platforms) to produce work that would have been impossible or 5x slower without them
Fluency with LLM concepts - you understand prompting, context windows, model tradeoffs, and can evaluate AI-powered features with judgment, not just enthusiasm
Experience evaluating where AI can create leverage in product features or internal workflows
Self-sufficient and resourceful - you do not wait to be told what to do, and you ask for what you need
You thrive managing multiple products simultaneously and context-switching without dropping balls
Bonus:
Experience in consumer products, marketplaces, or subscription businesses
Familiarity with SEO, paid acquisition, or growth marketing
Experience shipping AI-powered product features (recommendations, automation, NLP, agents, etc)
Side projects, demos, or prototypes that demonstrate hands-on AI building
Familiarity with eval frameworks, prompt iteration, or agentic workflows
Exposure to portfolio or multi-business environments
What Adly Offers
A leadership team that has founded, scaled, and exited multiple companies - with clear ambition to make Adly a dominant player in SaaS and content
Ownership across multiple businesses - not one feature team, but real P&L-level impact
Small team with significant room for career advancement
A work environment that is equal parts startup energy and results-driven meritocracy
Opportunities to grow your technical, analytical, and leadership skills rapidly
Health benefits (medical, dental, vision) - U.S. only
Unlimited PTO
