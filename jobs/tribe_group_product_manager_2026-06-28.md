About The Opportunity
This is a newly created Product Manager role with a leading representative professional services body. . The position has been created to bring dedicated leadership to the profitability, optimisation and growth of a portfolio of digital products, while identifying and launching new offerings that support long-term commercial sustainability.
You will take responsibility for product direction, working closely with colleagues across marketing, digital, business development, finance, and analytics to support and shape priorities, validate opportunities, build business cases, and deliver improvements from idea through to launch. 
With an eye on future growth, the role supports expansion of digital products and will suit someone who enjoys collaboration, operational independence, commercial accountability, and building in a fast-evolving digital landscape.
What You’ll Bring
Proven product management experience across the full lifecycle (idea to launch and iteration)
Strong digital product capability, including working with online platforms and data-led decisions
Commercial acumen: comfortable with pricing, financial modelling, and product profitability
Experience building business cases and prioritising a roadmap across multiple products
Confident relationship management across marketing, finance, digital and delivery partners
Comfortable operating independently in a newly created role 
Hands-on, pragmatic delivery mindset (not just strategy)
Background in professional services, regulated environments or financial services organisations could be beneficial.
What’s on Offer
Newly created role with genuine operational independence and end-to-end product leadership and development
Flexible work environment and opportunity to work your day between 7am and 7pm
Portfolio of digital products with clear commercial impact and visibility
Strong internal support across finance, marketing, digital and analytics/BA capability
Opportunity to build new products and take them to market, not just maintain BAU
Growth exposure, with products expanding beyond New Zealand
Collaborative team environment with a growth mindset
