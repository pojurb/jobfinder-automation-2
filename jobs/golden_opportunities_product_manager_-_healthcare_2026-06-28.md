### Full Job Description
Define and communicate product and portfolio strategy through roadmaps aligned to enterprise objectives, customer outcomes, OKRs/KPIs, and platform direction.
Own prioritization and trade-off decisions across portfolios and backlogs, balancing customer experience, business value, technical feasibility, cost, return on investment, operational readiness, and risk.
Drive alignment across teams to minimize overlapping work, manage dependencies, and coordinate integrated delivery for complex, cross-domain initiatives.
Partner closely with Product Manager to Translate complex business needs into capabilities, features, user stories, and acceptance criteria; ensure teams maintain a continuously “ready” backlog.
Partner closely with engineering, architects, and data teams on requirements definition, solution design, data flows, integration patterns, and architectural considerations.
Facilitate cross-functional decision-making in alignment with enterprise governance, business strategy, and technology standards; negotiate solutions to achieve win-win outcomes.
Establish and sustain stakeholder feedback loops (business, end users, operations) to inform roadmap evolution and continuous improvement.
Support financial and investment management for the capability/portfolio by partnering with finance and operations on CBA/ROI, funding, and benefits realization
