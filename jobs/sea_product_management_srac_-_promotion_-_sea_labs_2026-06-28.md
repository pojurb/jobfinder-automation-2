### Full Job Description
About the Team:
Sea Labs is at the core of the Sea platform development, supporting diverse business lines from e-commerce, supply chain, games, payment, and finance, among many others. The strong growth and unique positioning of Sea's e-commerce business, Shopee, spurred the launch of Sea Labs Indonesia. Since its inception, passionate engineers have charted the course to drive the best experience for our users in Indonesia, many of which solutions are even adapted to other regional markets.
Sea's hyper-growing business scale has transformed most "innocent" problems into huge technical challenges, and there is no better place to experience world-class projects first-hand if you love technologies as much as we do. Together with our passionate and driven teams, you'll get to develop your skills, build on industry knowledge, and collaborate with global teams in a dynamic space. Browse our Sea Labs Indonesia team openings to see how you can make an impact with us.
At Shopee Search & Recommendation, we connect users to what they may want at scale. As a pillar of e-commerce discovery experience, every day, our products enable tens of millions of users to discover relevant and interesting things among hundreds of millions of products and contents available on Shopee. We’re a passionate, young, and diverse team, committed to creating a convenient, delightful, and intelligent discovery experience for our users. We are looking for an (experienced) product manager to join us. You will be working closely with the product team to make significant impacts on Shopee's search & recommendation engine through both technical and operational processes.
Job Description:
Help own promotion features for Livestream & Video: Contributing from problem definition through launch and post-launch measurement.
Dig into data to understand how promotions perform: Write SQL, explore dashboards, segment users, and turn numbers into clear insights.
Support the design and analysis of experiments (A/B tests, holdouts) to measure whether a promotion drove real and incremental impact.
Diagnose problems systematically: decompose metrics, form hypotheses, and investigate root causes.
Help define the right success metrics for each initiative, considering both growth and healthy economics.
Collaborate with marketing, design, and other teams to deliver promotions that are effective and trusted by users.
Communicate findings clearly: Turning data and analysis into recommendations stakeholders can act on.
Requirements:
Bachelor's degree in a quantitative or technical field (Computer Science, Statistics, Engineering, Mathematics, Economics, or similar).
Have 1-3 years of working experience as product management; preferably in e-commerce / content platforms with content moderation, risk, or fraud background.
Working knowledge of SQL or a willingness to learn it fast.
Familiarity with A/B testing, experimentation, or basic statistics.
Good communication and proficiency in Bahasa Indonesia & English, both verbal and written. 
Strong ownership, able to proactively drive initiatives from ideation to execution.
Strong stakeholder management skills, able to effectively align and manage expectations across diverse stakeholders.
