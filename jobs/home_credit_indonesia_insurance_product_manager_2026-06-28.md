### Full Job Description
You will in a mission to drive the development and implementation of new digital insurance and protection products—focused primarily on accelerating the growth of stand‑alone insurance through the Telesales channel. Ensure these products deliver strong customer value and generate meaningful fee‑based income for HCID. It will covers:
What you will do:
Leads the development and growth of stand‑alone insurance products through the Telesales channel, working closely with the Telesales team to craft offerings, improve conversion, and scale revenue.
Develops new Stand Alone or bundling insurance / protection product concepts, product designs and features and tests them via our telesales and mobile apps channel
Collaborates actively with the Telesales and CRM teams to train, communicate, and continuously enhance insurance products and processes, ensuring strong sales readiness and consistent customer experience.
Cooperates with Pricing Manager to define the right pricing for new insurance / protection products and review it time to time
Provide regular reports and updates to management: insurance penetration, mis-selling, premium, SLAs, etc.
Reviews performance (sales, performance against plan) of existing insurance / protection products and related processes and proposes changes to improve customer experience and process simplicity internally
Provides briefs to Marketing Communication and Digital team to prepare campaign wording, BTL promotion materials, Online advertisement and in some case ATL campaigns. Reviews outcomes and suggests changes
Manages product setup in HCID core system and cooperates with IT on User Acceptance testing and ongoing management of the insurance / protection product setup in the system
Takes active part in resolution of customer complaints related to insurance / protection products, services and features design and processes
What you will need to have:
At least 4 years of working experience within Insurance company or insurance broker with 
insurance product development
 background is mandatory.
Preferably has product knowledge around Insurance product features and business development mindset.
Ability to analyze and report/communicate results together with root cause analysis and development of enhancements/corrective actions.
Experienced and exposed with planning, pricing and forecasting products and sales campaigns and analysis of effectiveness/ROI for campaigns.
Good presentation and communication skill in English both written and verbal,
A self-driven to get sourced and find opportunities and customer oriented.
Comfortable to work in agile, dynamic, and high demand environment.
Has exposure within Business Development and end-to-end Product Development activities within Insurance or insurance broker industry would be a good advantage.
