### Full Job Description
Product Manager – Payment Systems
Requirements :
Minimum 5–8 years of experience in Product Management, with at least 3 years in payment systems, fintech, banking, or financial services.
Strong understanding of the end-to-end payment ecosystem, including payment gateways, virtual accounts, QR payments, e-wallets, card payments, remittance, settlement, and reconciliation.
Proven experience defining product strategy, roadmap, and feature prioritization based on business objectives and customer needs.
Ability to translate business requirements into detailed product requirements and user stories for engineering teams.
Experience working closely with cross-functional teams, including Engineering, Design, QA, Operations, Compliance, and Business stakeholders.
Familiarity with APIs, system integrations, and technical concepts to effectively collaborate with development teams.
Strong analytical skills with experience using data and customer insights to drive product decisions.
Experience managing the product lifecycle from ideation to launch and continuous improvement.
Excellent communication, stakeholder management, and presentation skills.
Experience working in Agile/Scrum environments and using tools such as Jira or Confluence.
Preferred Qualifications
Experience launching or managing products in payments, digital banking, fintech, or merchant acquiring.
Understanding of payment industry standards and regulations, such as PCI DSS, ISO 8583, or ISO 20022.
Knowledge of fraud prevention, risk management, and transaction monitoring within payment systems.
Experience working with international payment methods, cross-border payments, or financial APIs is a plus.
MBA or relevant product management certifications (e.g., CSPO, Pragmatic, or equivalent) is an advantage.
