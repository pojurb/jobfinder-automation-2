Build What Matters. Grow Beyond Limits.
DOKU is where innovation meets real impact, creating seamless, secure payment experiences for millions, every day.
Job Title: 
Product Owner PayChat
About The Role: 
Due to the many additional features in PayChat that require development and end-to-end monitoring, as well as many activities using Jira and Confluence, a PO (Product Owner) is needed to handle PODS in the messaging service with the PayChat feature.
What Will You Do:
To ensure product requirements, and documentation are followed
To ensure products are in scope on schedule. 
Provide an active role and use critical judgment in the development of all product deliverables
 Ensure quality and timely submission of all product deliverables and documentation as assigned by Manager BaaS
Prepare with reporting on the product status. 
Prepare with monitoring quality assurance and change control processes. 
Collaboration with WhatsApp vendors. 
Coordination with WhatsApp Vendors regarding technical issues. 
Creating WhatsApp Product documentation. 
Conducting market research on the use of WhatsApp chatbots. 
Coordinating with WhatsApp Vendors in technical product development and problem-solving. 
Collaborating with WhatsApp Vendors to develop WhatsApp flow features for PayChat products. 
Creating comprehensive product documentation, including wireframes and product initiation designs, tailored to messaging service requirements. 
Developing and delivering professional product presentations to stakeholders
What we are looking for: 
A junior, senior, or graduate student currently enrolled in a program with a focus on product management or management in technology. 
A strong interest in product Management as a career is preferred
Have an academic level of written and verbal communication skills
Strong interpersonal skills to actively listen, communicate in ways that foster trust and show flexibility
Team spirit and the ability to work with a group of diverse people, provide recognition and reward
Ability to work in a fast-paced, self-directed environment under deadline pressures
Ability to manage own workload independently, multi-task, and prioritize effectively
High computer literacy
Soft Skill Requirements:
Two Ways Good Communication
Technical Skill Requirements:
Jira, Confluence, Clickup, Notion, Figma
