### Full Job Description
JoVE is the world-leading producer and provider of video solutions with the mission to improve scientific research and education. Millions of scientists, educators and students use JoVE for their research, teaching and learning. Our institutional clients comprise over 1,000 universities, colleges, and biopharma companies, including such leaders as Harvard, MIT, Yale, and Stanford. As a rapidly growing company, with offices in the USA, UK, Australia, and India servicing clients in over 60 countries, we are seeking talented and ambitious individuals to join our company. 
The role
We’re looking for a new kind of Product Manager—someone who doesn’t just define products but can 
prototype, test, and iterate independently using modern AI tools
.
This role reflects a shift in product management: from coordination-heavy execution to 
hands-on creation, rapid experimentation, and deep user understanding
. You will operate with high autonomy, combining product thinking, technical fluency, and strong taste to ship meaningful experiences faster.
What will you do:
Build prototypes, landing pages, and lightweight applications independently using modern no/low-code and AI-assisted tools
Rapidly validate product ideas using tools like Replit, Lovable, Claude Code, or similar platforms
Translate product concepts into working demos within hours or days—not weeks
Conduct frequent user interviews, usability testing, and feedback sessions with customers and prospects
Synthesize user feedback, product data, and intuition into clear, high-impact product opportunities
Drive intuitive, elegant user experiences with a strong focus on simplicity, quality, and attention to detail
Define metrics, analyze product usage data, and run experiments to inform product decisions
Leverage AI and automation tools to prototype features, streamline workflows, and increase team velocity
Clearly communicate product vision, priorities, and trade-offs across cross-functional stakeholders
Foster a high-performance culture built on ownership, speed, accountability, and high standards
Skills And Qualifications:
A Bachelor’s or Master’s degree
2–5 years of Product Management experience building products in EdTech, SaaS, HealthTech, marketplaces, or consumer technology companies
Strong user-centric mindset with experience conducting user research, gathering feedback, and translating insights into product decisions
Builder mentality with the ability to create prototypes, validate ideas quickly, and leverage AI-assisted or no-code tools independently
Analytical and data-driven approach with experience using SQL, product metrics, experimentation, and analytics tools to drive decisions
Technical fluency with a solid understanding of software systems, APIs, backend architectures, or microservices, plus familiarity with coding concepts and AI coding tools
Excellent communication, stakeholder management, and ownership mindset with the ability to move fast and operate effectively in ambiguous, fast-paced environments
Why Join JoVE?
A competitive compensation package.
You will make a direct impact in accelerating science research and education.
Opportunity to work with global teams and in an environment that promotes innovation and collaboration.
Our strong promotion from within culture draws a clear path to advance your career with us
