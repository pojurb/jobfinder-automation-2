### Full Job Description
Come join Credit Data Solutions for the 21st Century | Bloom Credit!
Bloom Credit
Bloom Credit is a modern API platform that redefines how credit data is accessed, reported, and used. We’re on a mission to simplify and modernize the credit ecosystem — making it easier for everyone, from startups to large banks, to report consumer-permissioned data (CPD) and launch innovative, accessible financial products.
Our flagship product, 
Bloom+
, enables financial institutions to report recurring consumer payments (like rent, utilities, and telecom bills) to major credit bureaus, helping consumers build credit profiles outside of traditional means. By expanding the scope of creditworthiness, we're helping organizations improve lives through responsible access to credit.
Our goal is simple: to help everyone from seed-stage startups to large, established banks (and everyone in between) access and furnish accurate credit data easily and without error; we believe this will lead to increased innovation and the creation of accessible, unique financial products for everyone
Our mission is backed by human-centric thinking paired with a modern approach to credit reporting and furnishment; we’re doing our part to rewrite the script of financial services by offering easy ways for financial services providers to create human-centric products that help people achieve their dreams
Senior Product Manager
Are you passionate about helping Financial Institutions empower consumers while growing their business? This is a full-time remote role for a Sr. Product Manager, who will focus on our Data Access product which enables financial institutions to leverage credit data, from credit scores and attributes to inform credit decisions to showing the credit score to the consumer.
This role will report to Christa Degnan | LinkedIn and join a small Product Management Team (CPO, 2 Senior Product Managers, and this role).
The Sr. Product Manager will oversee the product lifecycle, from ideation to launch, and ongoing product iteration based on user feedback and market trends. Responsibilities include defining product vision and strategy, prioritizing features, creating product roadmaps, and collaborating with cross-functional teams. Additionally, the Sr. Product Manager will analyze product performance metrics and ensure alignment with company objectives.
Data Access is at an inflection point where the bureau coverage is expanding, the product is moving upmarket into insights and AI, and there's a new consumer surface launching. It's a well-scoped product with high visibility and real near-term shipping opportunities, inside a company small enough that a Sr. PM will genuinely own outcomes.
Qualifications
5 years of experience in Product Management, defining product vision and strategy, and creating product roadmap
Experienced in building credit data solutions
API-first architecture; ideally experience with credit data/decisioning or experience building or owning a data product that went from raw data delivery to layered insights
Proficiency in collaborating with cross-functional teams including engineering, design, and marketing
Skills in market research and analyzing product performance metrics
Excellent communication and leadership skills
Ability to work independently and remotely
Experience with API products and the fintech industry is a plus
Bachelor's degree in business, Computer Science, Engineering, or related field
