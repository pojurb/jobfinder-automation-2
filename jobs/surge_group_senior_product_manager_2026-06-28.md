### Full Job Description
Principal / Senior Principal Product Manager
Location:
 Remote (US) | West Coast Preferred
About the Company
Our client is one of the world's top three stablecoin issuers, operating at the forefront of digital payments, blockchain infrastructure, and onchain finance.
The company provides technology that enables the seamless transfer of digital value across blockchain networks, supporting developers, businesses, and financial institutions worldwide. Its products help power payments, commerce, and financial applications at a global scale, playing an important role in the continued growth and adoption of digital assets.
The Opportunity
We are looking for a Principal or Senior Principal Product Manager to join a high-impact team focused on building the next generation of onchain infrastructure.
This is a rare opportunity to own strategy, vision, and execution for products that sit at the centre of the crypto ecosystem. You'll work closely with developers, partners, and internal stakeholders to identify emerging opportunities, launch new initiatives, and drive adoption across a global network of users and builders.
The ideal candidate combines exceptional product leadership with deep blockchain expertise and a genuine passion for the future of onchain finance.
What You'll Be Doing
Define product strategy, vision, and roadmap for new onchain initiatives
Identify opportunities that accelerate adoption and usage across the ecosystem
Work directly with developers, partners, and ecosystem participants to understand market needs
Translate technical innovation into products that deliver meaningful user and business impact
Analyse onchain activity, user behaviour, and market trends to inform product decisions
Lead products from concept through launch and ongoing optimisation
Partner closely with engineering, legal, data, marketing, and commercial teams
Improve the end-to-end experience for developers and users interacting with blockchain infrastructure
Represent the company within the wider crypto ecosystem and contribute to industry best practices
What We're Looking For
5+ years of product management experience with ownership of complex commercial products
Experience operating at Senior, Lead, Group, Principal, or equivalent Product Manager level
Strong technical understanding with the ability to work closely alongside engineering teams
Proven experience defining product strategy and driving execution in fast-moving environments
Highly analytical mindset with a data-driven approach to decision making
Demonstrated ability to influence senior stakeholders and lead cross-functional initiatives
Strong communication skills and the ability to simplify complex technical concepts
Preferred Experience
Experience building within crypto, blockchain, or Web3 environments
Deep understanding of stablecoins, wallets, DeFi, smart contracts, bridges, and digital asset infrastructure
Experience launching and scaling blockchain-based products
Founder, early-stage startup, or entrepreneurial experience
Background contributing to developer communities, open-source projects, or ecosystem initiatives
Familiarity with blockchain infrastructure and emerging onchain technologies
Why Apply?
This role offers the opportunity to shape products that are helping define the future of digital finance. You'll work alongside some of the industry's strongest product, engineering, and blockchain talent while building technology used by millions globally.
If you're passionate about crypto, thrive in highly technical environments, and enjoy turning ambitious ideas into widely adopted products, we'd love to hear from you.
