### Full Job Description
The job holder is responsible for managing overall (Company Level) Value Added Services / Insurance product programs, estimating future profit & loss on new and existing portfolios, developing product roadmap / strategy and finding business / partnership opportunities. Holds responsibility and accountability for managing the risk/return tradeoffs for the businesses, and interacting with stakeholders regarding Value Added Services business health tracking.
Main Responsibilities:
Provide direction on new insurance product program development, both financing-bundled and standalone, that drive revenue and profitability for the company.
Plan / estimate performance of all existing and new products or services.
Evaluate / review product programs especially but not limited to on their sales performance and customer experience metrics
Ensure good governance and compliance of insurance business, product and program according to both internal and external policies / regulations.
Supervise and review product approval process (PAP) and all relevant documents related to it
Develop and manage team members
Requirements:
University degree in Management, Business, or other bachelor degree
Minimum of 10+ years proven experience in insurance product Sr. Manager position, Banking or Consumer Finance background, Excellent knowledge and practical experience in strategy, processes, procedures, portfolio analysis and reporting, advanced analytical thinking and decision making
Minimum of 10+ years in insurance product.
